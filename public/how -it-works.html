How it works for Clients?

wooqlaw connects you to lawyers based on your search criteria from anywhere, at anytime to help you find the best solution to your legal issue.
1.
To get started:
Either click within the search box and select the legal practice area from the list, or select a lawyers location from the list
2.
Filter listings according to your needs:
Next, use the detailed search filters on the lawyers list to narrow down your search results and find the most suitable lawyers according to your requirements search filters to narrow down your result set for the most suitable lawyers as per your requirement.
3.
Filter listings according to your needs:
Select the “Online” search filter and use a combination of options to list lawyers who are currently online and available
4.
Are you looking for a female lawyer to help you with your legal issue?
Click within the search box and select the legal practice area, then use the “Gender” search filter on the lawyer listing screen to locate a female lawyer. Select city, court, languages and other search criteria to further narrow down lawyer profiles
Combine city, court, languages and other search filters to further narrow down lawyer profiles
5.
Need a fixed consultation time?
After you have finalised your list of lawyer profiles, select “Next Available Appointment” or go to your selected lawyer’s “Book Appointment” section in order to book a fixed appointment time

Submitting your legal inquiry for quotation!

You have two options to submit your legal inquiry for quotation:
Submit inquiry to your selected lawyer
Apply the desired search criteria to list lawyer profiles, then go to the selected lawyer's profile and submit your inquiry using 'Request Quotation' function.
Submit inquiry to premium lawyers that match your criteria
Use submit inquiry to premium lawyers feature (on the main screen). Your inquiry will be sent to all lawyers (confidentially) that match your criteria. You should expect multiple responses from the interested lawyers with pricing and details.
